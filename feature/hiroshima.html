Atomic Bombing of Hiroshima

Triptych, 2019

This triptych depicts a chosen recognizable event in human history. I chose the atomic bombing of Hiroshima as it was a significant, and tragic, moment in history.

The three panels would relay the event chronologically from what happened right before the bombing, during, and moments after. Each panel also had a central visual focus — the leftmost employing only typography, the center using imagery, and the right using symbols.

The goal was to tell the story of the bombing and evoke the feelings of loss, grief, chaos, and destruction. These emotions are shown through the use of dark colours, graphic imagery, and strong language in the form of real quotes sourced from the victims of the attack.
