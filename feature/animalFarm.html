Animal Farm

Book Jacket Design, 2020

This is a book jacket designed with a unique identity for a chosen novel, which was Animal Farm by George Orwell. The project took an experimental approach to typography as the jacket had to be hand-lettered/crafted, constructed, or photographed. The final piece was a physical mock-up of the jacket wrapped around an actual hardcover book.

My goal for this book jacket is to use distressed industrial materials to highlight the corruption of power within the story of Animal Farm. The cover is made out of corrugated cardboard with ripped pieces of paper and black acrylic paint for added depth and grime. The title and headings are hand-painted using red acrylic paint mixed with black ink. The typography is based on the cardboard's corrugation, as it is difficult to paint in other styles that go against the grain of the corrugation.
