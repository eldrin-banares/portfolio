End LGBTQ+ Misrepresentation in Media

Kinetic Typography, 2019

This is the 4D piece that is part of a semester-long series of projects that addresses fundamental issues in a social cause of our choosing. Choosing LGBTQ+ awareness is personal because it is a community I belong to and, through the use of design and different media, I could shed light on its different issues.

The goal of this short video is to showcase the diversity of the members within the gay community.

Through my preliminary research, I find that they are misrepresented in current mainstream media by being stereotyped and generalized into a single "type" of gay person. By using Adobe AfterEffects, I created an active, colourful, and dynamic video that focused primarily on kinetic typography with supporting imagery.
