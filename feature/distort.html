Distort

Conceptual Exhibition Catalogue, 2019

For this project, I was tasked to develop a creative direction for a type-based exhibition catalogue that reflects the characteristics of a chosen designer or artist.

The exhibition catalogue is dedicated to sharing the visual works of the makeup artist and drag performer, Hungry. They are famous on Instagram (@isshehungry) for sharing their intricate facial and body makeup, costumes, and set designs.

This catalogue, titled DISTORT, is an extension of Hungry’s extraordinary ability to distort their appearance. The catalogue’s pages appear normal at first, but an important feature of each page is that each can be opened to reveal a bigger spread containing more text and imagery. By doing so, not only are the imagery of Hungry warped and extended, but traditional layouts and typography are distorted as well.
